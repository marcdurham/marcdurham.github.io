--- layout: post title: The Coldest Cold date: '2016-02-14T01:38:00.001-08:00' author: Marc Durham tags: modified_time: '2016-02-14T01:38:41.407-08:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-6813567.post-3061054700125966022 --- Not more than ten minutes ago I was outside, walking home from the bus stop, in the coldest cold I've ever felt.

They often post a "Wind chill" temperature too, which seems kinda silly sometimes, but I haven't seen one.  It's not really windy, it's just a light breeze.  But my hands are still burning as they thaw out.  Apparently, according to a weather website, the temperature is only -5 degrees F, merely.  We've had -25 degrees many times since I've been here, but I've never felt so cold in my life.  My skin burned, I took my hands out to answer my phone.  It was less than a minute conversation, but my hands still hurt from it.
